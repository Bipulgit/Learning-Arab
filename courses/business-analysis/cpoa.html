Course Description

The CPOA course provides a comprehensive understanding of property management principles, covering topics such as property leasing, tenant management, property maintenance, financial record-keeping, and compliance with local property laws and regulations. Through this course, candidates learn essential skills, including lease agreement management, tenant communication, maintenance scheduling, budgeting, and financial reporting. The curriculum is designed to provide participants with a balanced blend of theoretical knowledge and practical applications relevant to property management, giving them the tools to support property owners effectively.

About exam & certification details:

👉 What is the Process to Get CPOA Certified ?

Step 1. Complete a CPOA Preparation Course.
Step 2. Prepare for the Exam.
Step 3. Take the Exam.

👉 What is the Exam Fee for CPOA Exam?

The CPOA exam fee typically ranges between $200 and $300 USD depending on the certifying organization and location. Certain training programs may also include the exam fee as part of the course package.

👉 What is the Eligibility Criteria for CPOA Certification ?

Work Experience: Some certifying bodies may require candidates to have prior experience in property management or a related field, though entry-level candidates may also apply if the program is intended for beginners.
Education: While not mandatory, having a high school diploma or equivalent is often recommended to better understand the curriculum.

👉 How Long is the CPOA Exam Duration ?

The CPOA certification exam duration is usually between 2 to 3 hours, allowing sufficient time for candidates to answer multiple-choice questions covering the core areas of property management.
