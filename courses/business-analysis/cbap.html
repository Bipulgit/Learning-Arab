About exam & certification details:

👉 What are the Benefits of CBAP Certification?

1. Recognition of advanced business analysis skills.
2. Improved career opportunities and higher earning potential.
3. Access to a global network of professionals and resources.
4. Credibility in the industry, which can help with leadership and strategic decision-making roles.

👉 What do I Need to Become a CBAP®?

1. Complete a minimum of 7,500 hours of Business Analysis Work experience in the last 10 years.
2. Within this experience, a minimum of 900 hours completed in 4 of the 6 BABOK® Guide Knowledge Areas, for a total of at least 3,600 of the required 7,500 total.
3. Complete a minimum of 35 hours of Professional development in the last 4 years.
4. Provide two references.
5. Agree to Code of Conduct.
6. Agree to Terms and Conditions.
7. Pass the exam.

👉 What is the Process to Get CBAP Certified ?

Step 1. Meet Eligibility Requirements.
Step 2. Apply for the Exam.
Step 3. Prepare and Take the Exam.

👉 What is the Exam Fee for CBAP Exam?

The CBAP exam fee is $450 USD for IIBA members and $575 USD for non-members. Additionally, there is a non-refundable application fee of $125 USD that is required at the time of application submission.

👉 What is the Eligibility Criteria for CBAP Certification ?

Work Experience: A minimum of 7,500 hours of work experience in business analysis over the last 10 years.
Knowledge Areas: Within the 7,500 hours, at least 900 hours should be in four of the six BABOK knowledge areas.
Professional Development: A minimum of 35 hours of professional development in business analysis in the past four years.
References: Two references from a career manager, client, or Certified Business Analysis Professional.

👉 How Long is the CBAP Exam Duration ?

The CBAP certification exam duration is 3.5 hours (210 minutes), and it consists of 120 multiple-choice questions. These questions are case study-based and require analysis of real-world scenarios.
