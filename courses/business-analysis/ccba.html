Course Description

The CCBA course provides a comprehensive introduction to the essential concepts, techniques, and processes involved in business analysis. The course is designed to prepare individuals for the CCBA certification exam by covering the Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK).

ðŸ‘‰ What is the Exam Fee for CCBA Exam?

The CCBA exam fee is approximately $325 USD for IIBA members and $450 USD for non-members. The fees may vary slightly depending on the region or any discounts available.

ðŸ‘‰ What is the Eligibility Criteria for CCBA Certification ?

Business Analysis Experience: A minimum of 3,750 hours of business analysis work experience in the past 7 years, which is equivalent to approximately 2.5 years of full-time work.
Education: A high school diploma or equivalent and at least 21 hours of professional development in business analysis (completed in the last 4 years).
BABOK Knowledge: Knowledge of the Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK Guide) is essential for the exam.

ðŸ‘‰ How Long is the CCBA Exam Duration ?

The CCBA certification exam lasts 3.5 hours, and candidates are required to answer 130 multiple-choice questions.
